## Cleverbase

### About

At Cleverbase, we help make digital trust a practical reality by building secure, user-friendly solutions for digital identification and signing. Founded in 2016, we were created in response to the growing need for reliable electronic identity in an increasingly digital society. We have shown that fully digital identification can be done safely and in line with eIDAS, and we continue to contribute to the development of the European digital trust landscape, including the move toward eIDAS 2.0. Today, more than a quarter of a million people have completed over one million secure online transactions with Cleverbase, and we support users across all European nationalities. Our work is driven by a clear mission: to make digital processes faster, simpler, and more affordable, while remaining accessible and reliable. We do this through close collaboration between developers, lawyers, security experts, and designers, combining deep expertise with an agile, domain-driven approach to deliver meaningful improvements in a highly regulated environment.

## D-Trust

### About

As an independent and qualified trust service provider, D-Trust has been listed with the Bundesnetzagentur under the eIDAS Regulation since 2016. We translate trust into concrete products: We provide legally compliant and certified trust services such as digital certificates and electronic signatures, as well as solutions for secure data management such as trusted data platforms. They meet the highest security standards of modern infrastructures and enable secure digital identities for companies, public authorities, and in the private sphere.

### About

Digitel TS develops infrastructure and services for digital identity, electronic signatures, and trusted digital transactions in regulated environments in the spanish market. Our work focuses on implementing systems aligned with European trust frameworks such as eIDAS and the upcoming European Digital Identity Wallet ecosystem.  By combining expertise in identity protocols, cryptography, and secure system architecture, we help organizations integrate compliant trust services into their digital platforms. ## Docaposte

### About

Docaposte, the digital subsidiary of La Poste Group and a leader in digital trust, supports companies and public institutions in their transformation in three priority markets: the public sector, healthcare and finance. 
As a publisher of software and digital trust solutions, a platform integrator and operator, and an expert in data-IA consulting, Docaposte draws on its own technology bricks and its assets in the cloud, cybersecurity and AI to design, develop and operate complete customer-centric digital journeys, while complying with the most demanding regulations and ensuring highly secure data.
As the leader in trust solutions (electronic signature and voting, digital archiving…), and the leading operator of healthcare data (with over 49 million of medical records) and school data (18 million of PRONOTE’s users), Docaposte has a unique market positioning combining technological sovereignty and public interest, and is committed to ethics, responsibility and accessibility for all.

## Evrotrust Technologies

### About

Evrotrust is a European qualified trust service provider (QTSP) offering eIDAS-compliant digital identity and trust services. Its platform combines EU-notified electronic identification at high assurance with qualified electronic signatures, seals, timestamps, eDelivery, validation, and preservation services. Businesses can integrate Evrotrust via API, SDK, or portal for remote onboarding, signing, and compliance workflows, while Evrotrust bears the legal responsibility for identification as a QTSP.
