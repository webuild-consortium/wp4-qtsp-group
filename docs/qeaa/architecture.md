# High-level architecture for QeAA in WE BUILD

## Context

### Purpose of this document

This document specifies the high-level architecture for the issuance and validation of qualified electronic attestations of attributes (QeAA) within WE BUILD.
It complements the [WE BUILD architecture documentation](https://github.com/webuild-consortium/architecture) which specifies, among other topics, the issuance and validation of attestations within WE BUILD in general.

### Definitions and legal requirements

In WE BUILD, QTSPs as defined under [eIDAS](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX%3A02014R0910-20241018) Art. 3(20) provide pre-production eAA issuance and validation services as defined under Art. 3(16)(g) and (h), ready to be qualified as defined under Art. 3(17), for QeAA as defined under Art. 3(45).
