# Feature: QERDS between wallets

The sender and recipient each have a business wallet.

## Scenario: Data transmission

One business wallet user uses QERDS to transmit data to another business wallet.

## Scenario: Sending data

The business wallet uses its QERDS provider to submit a document or to create a notification.

## Scenario: Receiving data

The business wallet uses its QERDS provider to receive a document or notification.
